Who Said The Slippers Can't Be Stylish?

In fact, our women's slippers are a proof that slippers can be stylish, unique and suitable for both indoor and outdoor use.

The soft velour quilted clog slippers for women have a playful faux fur lining that makes them fashionable and cute.

These cozy house slippers can be worn by women of all ages, so get them for yourself or as a gift for any woman or girl you know!

The Women's Slippers That Combine Style And Comfort

We have designed the best looking and, at the same, the most comfortable fluffy slippers that you'll ever come across!

The lady slippers are made memory foam which provides support and unbelievable comfort to the feet and legs.

Plus, the thermoplastic rubber sole will allow you to walk with confidence, because there's no chance that you'll slip when wearing these slippers.

Pick The Color That Matches Your Personality

The comfortable and stylish soft velour quilted clog slippers for women come in 6 different colors to choose from:

  • Navy
  • Gray
  • Brown
  • Black
  • Pink
  • Purple

Every single color will look great with any pyjamas or clothes you wear, so pick the one the matches your personality!

Time To Pick Out Your Size

We hope you know what size shoes you wear, because it's time to pick the right size for your new cute house slippers! The sizes available are:

  • Small (5-6)
  • Medium (7-8)
  • Large (9-10)
  • X-Large (11-12)

Have you picked the right size? Great! Because now it's time to place an order and make your feet warm and happy!
