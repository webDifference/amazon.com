Amazon Inspire is an open collaboration service that helps educators discover, gather, and share educational content.

Your destination for K-12 educational resources

  • Discover

    New resources are constantly being added. Search by title, subject, grade level, resource type, publisher, and standards.

  • Gather

    Download resources and collections shared by other educators and use them in your classrooms.

  • Share

    Upload and share your own content. Rate and review content so others can quickly find the best resources.

How Amazon Inspire Works

Share your content

Upload and share curriculum resources that you’ve either created or are authorized to share with educators everywhere, and help grow this library of digital educational resources from teachers, school districts, open educational resource (OER) providers, and publishers from across the country.
