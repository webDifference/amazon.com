Signature Stories

Signature Stories is a new program bringing the characters kids already know and love to original short stories in Amazon Rapids. Amazon Studio is the first to join Signature Stories, bringing new stories from the Amazon Original hit children's TV shows, Niko and the Sword of Light and Danger & Eggs, featuring the voice talent of Aidy Bryant (Saturday Night Live) and Tom Kenny (SpongeBob SquarePants).

How does it work?

  • Hundreds of Original Stories

    Explore our expanding library of hundreds of exclusive titles. We add dozens of new stories every month.

  • Read Along

    Read along with Amazon Rapids and a cast of lifelike character voices.

  • Parent Dashboard

    Learn about your reader's latest activity, and use a story summary to spark up a conversation.

  • Built-in Glossary

    Kids can easily look up the definition and pronunciation of a word to help build their reading confidence.
